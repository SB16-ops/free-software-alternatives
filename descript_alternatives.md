# Best Free Alternatives to Descript (Audio/Video Editing)

## Introduction
Users love Descript for its innovative approach to audio/video editing, but even with its popularity, a significant portion of users are searching for free alternatives that offer similar features without the hefty price tag. If you're one of them, look no further! In this article, we'll dive into the top 3 free alternatives to Descript, highlighting their key features and why they're great budget choices.

## The Top 3 Free Alternatives

### 1. Audacity

* **Key Features:** Multi-track recording, editing, and mixing; Effects like reverb, echo, and compression; Support for VST plugins
* **Why it's great:** Audacity is a powerful and feature-rich audio editor that offers more than just the basics. Its multi-track capabilities make it perfect for podcasters, musicians, or anyone who needs to work with multiple audio tracks.

### 2. Lightworks

* **Key Features:** Professional-level video editing; Support for HD and 4K resolutions; Multi-camera editing
* **Why it's great:** Lightworks is a professional-grade video editor that offers many of the same features as Descript, but at no cost. Its multi-camera editing capabilities make it perfect for filmmakers or YouTubers who need to work with multiple camera angles.

### 3. Shotcut

* **Key Features:** Open-source video editor; Support for HD and 4K resolutions; Multi-format support (AVI, MP4, FLV, etc.)
* **Why it's great:** Shotcut is a free and open-source video editor that offers many of the same features as Descript. Its multi-format support makes it perfect for creators who work with different file formats.

If you're tired of shelling out money for audio/video editing software, there are plenty of free alternatives to choose from!
