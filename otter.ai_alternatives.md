**Best Free Alternatives to Otter.ai (AI Meeting Assistant)**

### Introduction
---------------

Otter.ai is a popular AI-powered meeting assistant that helps users capture and organize meeting notes, summaries, and action items with ease. However, its premium features come at a cost, leaving many users searching for free alternatives that can deliver similar results without breaking the bank.

### The Top 3 Free Alternatives
-------------------------------

#### 1. Otter's Open-Source Alternative: Deepnote

* Key Features:
	+ AI-powered meeting note-taking with automated summarization
	+ Supports multiple file formats, including Google Docs and Microsoft Word
	+ Integrates with popular productivity apps like Slack and Trello
* Why it's great: As the open-source alternative to Otter.ai, Deepnote offers a similar feature set at no cost. Its integration with popular productivity tools makes it an excellent choice for teams.

#### 2. SimpleNote's Meeting Assistant: Simplenote

* Key Features:
	+ AI-powered meeting note-taking with automatic summarization
	+ Supports tagging and searching for easy organization
	+ Integrates with other SimpleNote features, like collaboration and version history
* Why it's great: Simplenote is a reliable note-taking app that offers a meeting assistant feature at no extra cost. Its focus on simplicity and ease of use makes it an excellent choice for individuals.

#### 3. VoiceNote: The AI-Powered Meeting Assistant

* Key Features:
	+ AI-powered meeting transcription with automated summarization
	+ Supports voice-to-text dictation for hands-free note-taking
	+ Integrates with popular calendar apps like Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook
* Why it's great: VoiceNote is a dedicated meeting assistant that leverages AI to deliver accurate transcriptions. Its voice-to-text feature makes it an excellent choice for users who prefer spoken notes.
