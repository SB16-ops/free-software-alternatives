## The Top 3 Free Alternatives

### 1. Trello
**Key Features:**
	+ Visual boards with Kanban-style workflow management
	+ Unlimited boards, lists, and cards
	+ Integrates with Google Drive, Dropbox, and more
	+ Mobile apps for on-the-go access

Why it's great: Trello offers a flexible, visual approach to project management that's easy to grasp. Its free plan is robust enough to handle small teams or personal projects.

### 2. Asana
**Key Features:**
	+ Task assignments with due dates and dependencies
	+ Customizable workflows for tailored processes
	+ Integration with Google Drive, Box, and more
	+ Mobile apps for task management on-the-go

Why it's great: Asana focuses on workflow management, making it perfect for teams that want to streamline their tasks and deadlines. Its free plan is suitable for small projects or individual users.

### 3. Wrike
**Key Features:**
	+ Gantt charts and customizable workflows
	+ Task assignments with dependencies and due dates
	+ Time tracking and reporting features
	+ Integration with Google Drive, Dropbox, and more

Why it's great: Wrike is an all-in-one project management tool that offers a robust feature set at no cost. Its free plan is ideal for small teams or individual users who want a comprehensive PM solution.
